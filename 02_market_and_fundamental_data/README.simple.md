## What is Market & Fundamental Data? ğŸ¯

Imagine you're trying to decide which baseball team will win the championship. You'd want to know:
- **Current scores** (Are they winning right now?)
- **Player stats** (How good are the players?)
- **Team records** (Have they been good this season?)
- **Stadium conditions** (Is it raining? That matters!)

**Market Data** is like the current score - it shows what's happening RIGHT NOW in trading (prices, how many people are buying/selling).

**Fundamental Data** is like the player stats - it shows how healthy a company is (how much money they make, if they have debts, etc.).

Together, they help you make smart decisions about which "teams" (companies) to invest in!
