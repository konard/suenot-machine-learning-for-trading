## What is Alpha Mining?

Imagine you're playing a treasure hunt game. In this game, you need to find clues that tell you where treasure (money) might be hidden. In the stock market and crypto world, these clues are called **"alpha factors"** or just **"alpha"**.

**Alpha** is like a secret recipe that tells you:
- When to buy something (like Bitcoin or Apple stock)
- When to sell something
- How to make more money than just randomly guessing
